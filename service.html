Service and Administration: Sanjeeva Balasuriya



Selected Roles

  • Elected Board Member, Australian Mathematical Sciences Institute.
  • Director (a.k.a. Associate Dean) Learning and Teaching, Faculty of Engineering, Computer and Mathematical Sciences, University of Adelaide (2020-2022).
  • Interim Academic Co-Director, Haide College (joint college between the University of Adelaide and Ocean University, China) (2021-2022).
  • Associate Head (Internationalisation), School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Adelaide (2020-).


Selected Outreach

  • Judge, Mathematics and Physics Section, Global Undergraduate Awards (2022-).
  • Governing Council Member, Glenunga International High School, Adelaide (2020-22).
  • Mathematician in Schools, in partnership with Sybila Ford (2016) and Anastasia Agorastos (2017-2018) of Norwood Primary School, Adelaide.
  • Mentor, Student Robotics Club of South Australia (2017-), in mentoring their middle-school "RoboRoos" teams for the FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Lego League (FLL) competitions. Their successes include:
    • First Place in Robot Programming, World FLL competition (Detroit, USA, April 2019)
    • First Place in Innovative Research Solution, World FLL competition (Houston, USA, April 2018)
    • Champion, Australian National FLL competition (Sydney, Australia, December 2017)
    • Champion, Australian Southeast Regional FLL competition (Melbourne, Australia, December 2021)
    • Second Place, Australian National FLL competition (Sydney, Australia, December 2018)
    • Champion, South Australian State FLL competition (Adelaide, Australia, November 2017)
  • Presenter, Mathematical Association of South Australia Annual Teaching Conference, Glenunga International School, Adelaide (Apr 2015).
  • Volunteer for the Aboriginal Summer School for Excellence in Technology and Science (ASSETS) program, Adelaide, Australia (2016-).
  • Presenter, Talented Students Day, organized by the Mathematical Association of New South Wales, University of Sydney (July 2003).
  • Presenter, Workshop on Learning Assessment, Postgraduate Institute of Science, University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka (Feb 1999).
  • Presenter, International Workshop on Science Education, Postgraduate Institute of Science, University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka (Jan 1999).
  • Session Organiser and Presenter, School Science Program, Institute of Fundamental Studies, Kandy, Sri Lanka (Nov 1997).
