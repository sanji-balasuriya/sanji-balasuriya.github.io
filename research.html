Sanjeeva Balasuriya: Publications

sanjeevabalasuriya@yahoo.com

I am interested in problems which lie at overlaps of pure mathematics, applied mathematics, statistics, data, and applications. As an applied analyst and mathematical modeller, I have in the past both developed methods for, and used methods in, disparate areas such as dynamical systems; geophysical and microfluidic fluid mechanics; ecology; optimization; stochastic modelling and simulation; control theory; cell and tumour growth modelling; functional analysis; combustion; regression methods; ordinary, partial and stochastic differential equations.
